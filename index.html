Annapurna Circuit Trek

Overview: The Annapurna Circuit Trek is a classic trekking route that circles the stunning Annapurna Massif. Known for its diverse landscapes, from subtropical forests to alpine terrain, and the highest point of the trek, the Thorong La Pass (5,416 meters), this trek is a favorite among trekkers worldwide. It offers an opportunity to experience the rich cultural heritage of the Gurung and Thakali communities, as well as views of Annapurna I, Dhaulagiri, and Machapuchare (Fishtail). Highlights: Traverse the Thorong La Pass, one of the highest trekking passes in the world. Explore the charming village of Manang, with its Tibetan culture and spectacular views. Witness the contrasting landscapes, from lush valleys to barren high-altitude deserts. Visit Muktinath Temple, a sacred pilgrimage site for Hindus and Buddhists. Relax in the natural hot springs at Tatopani. Breathtaking views of Annapurna, Dhaulagiri, Manaslu, and Machapuchare. Key Details: Duration: 12 Days Difficulty Level: Moderate to Challenging Best Season: March-May, September-November Altitude Range: 800 meters (Besisahar) to 5,416 meters (Thorong La Pass) Price: $1000 (Includes accommodation, guide, permits, and meals during the trek) Itinerary Summary: Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu to Besisahar, trek to Bhulbhule Day 2-4: Bhulbhule to Manang via Chamje and Pisang Day 5-6: Acclimatization at Manang, trek to Yak Kharka Day 7-8: Yak Kharka to Thorong Phedi and cross Thorong La Pass Day 9-10: Descend to Muktinath and trek to Jomsom Day 11-12: Trek to Tatopani and drive back to Pokhara

Duration: 12 Days

Price: $1000

Kathmandu Cultural Tour

Duration: 3 Days

Overview: The Kathmandu Cultural Tour is a deep dive into the rich history, art, and spirituality of Nepal's capital city. This tour takes you through UNESCO World Heritage Sites, including ancient temples, palaces, and stupas that are central to both Hinduism and Buddhism. It's perfect for travelers who want to explore the cultural heart of Nepal and gain insight into its vibrant traditions. Highlights: Visit Kathmandu Durbar Square, an ancient palace complex that showcases exquisite Newari architecture. Explore the sacred Hindu temple of Pashupatinath, one of the holiest temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Admire the awe-inspiring Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple), a stupa that offers panoramic views of Kathmandu Valley. Experience the serene beauty of Boudhanath Stupa, one of the largest stupas in the world and a center for Tibetan Buddhism. Discover Patan and Bhaktapur, ancient cities renowned for their artistic heritage, intricately carved wood and metalwork, and historic monuments. Key Details: Duration: 3 Days Difficulty Level: Easy Best Season: Year-round Price: $250 (Includes transportation, guide, and entry fees) Itinerary Summary: Day 1: Kathmandu Durbar Square, Swayambhunath, and Patan Durbar Square Day 2: Boudhanath Stupa, Pashupatinath Temple, and Bhaktapur Durbar Square Day 3: Visit Kirtipur, Bungamati, and explore local markets and artisan workshops

Price: $250
